Ching-Chia Renn

Ching-Chia Renn is multidisciplinary designer merging design, fabrication, and technology.

Ching-Chia has has an MDES in Design from the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia, and has hybrid design practices including art installation, mechanical design, spatial design, interaction design and product design.

2024 Emerging Bamboo Industry Focus Forum

空間裝置設計製作

Exhibition curation / Spatial Design

11 / 2024

此專案為「2024新興竹產業焦點論壇 Emerging Bamboo Industry Focus Forum」 (news) 設計展品展區之空間規劃。結合實際竹材於展覽空間中，展現竹子的堅毅與柔和，並呈現如同踏入竹林小徑之氛圍。

.

  • Client | Taiwan Institute of Economic Research (台灣經濟研究院)
  • Content | Exhibition spatial design (including design, manufacturing, and installing)
  • Site | GIS MOTC Convention Center, Taipei
  • Design Partner | Po-Yu LIN
  • Material | bamboo, steel, wood
