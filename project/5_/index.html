2022 - 2024

Endless Flowing Stream 《川流不息》

“Endless Flowing Stream” is inspired by two elements: “nature” and “flowing water”. The work produces a natural rows of the installation distributed along the wall flip as water flows over them, with an organic yet orderly feeling, flowing naturally downward. As the water alternately spreads out and coalesces, the visual image forms ripples, reflecting the sunlight. The shifting water, light, shadow and refracted colors echo the artistic spirit of impressionism.
